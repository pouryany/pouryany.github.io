I currently pursuing my PhD degree in computer science at UNC Charlotte under supervision of Dr. M.T. Mostafavi. Before that I got my bachelor in computer science from Sharif University of Technology.

My research spans around applications of network analysis algorithms in systems biology. In particular, I am working on subnetwork discovery algorithms for genomic networks in cancer, especially in ovarian cancer.

Teaching


  • Teaching Assistant -- Computer Architecture and Digital Design: Spring 2016 - Current
  • Instructor -- Design and Analysis of Algorithms: Fall 2015
  • Workshop Instructor and Organizer-- Summer School of Computer Science and Game Theory, Sharif University of Technology

Research Projects



Network Design in Analysis of Genomic Networks: Working on using design of analytical systems that utilize network design algorithm to find differentially expressed subnetworks in cancers.

Early Detection of Ovarian Serous Carcinoma: Worked on genomic analysis methods on Microarray data from ovarian cancer patients to find early detection biomarkers in ovarian cancer.
