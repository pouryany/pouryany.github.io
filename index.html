Pourya Naderi

Bio

I am a postdoctral research fellow at Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center and Harvard Medical School. My research focuses on network and pathway models for analysis of genomic data in complex diseases. Currently, I am working on models for understanding the role of non-coding RNAs in resilience to Alzheimer’s disease (AD) and finding potent therapeutic interventions that can alter pathway activity of AD.
